About Beacon Digital Solutions

Taylor Miller founded Beacon Digital Solutions in Palm Beach, FL, leveraging over a decade of expertise in project management, digital transformation, and agile methodologies.

Proven Expertise

Taylor Miller has delivered transformative results across diverse sectors. As a consultant with Booz Allen Hamilton, Taylor established an Agile Engineering and Test Automation Services Center of Excellence for the USCIS Office of Information Technology as well as launching the agency’s first annual Product Owner Summit in Crystal City, Arlington, VA. At Pratt & Whitney’s Military Engines division under RTX, Taylor crafted a digital transformation strategy and, as Product Owner, led the digitization of critical business processes, earning numerous awards for innovation and collaboration. Enhancing a family-owned commercial refrigeration business with growth strategies further refined Taylor’s operational skills. Certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP) and Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), Taylor blends strategic insight with actionable outcomes.

Driving Transformation

Beacon Digital Solutions embodies Taylor Miller’s vision of transforming challenges into opportunities through tailored, results-oriented strategies that foster innovation and operational excellence.
